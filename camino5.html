My Goal


To become a successful songwriter and have songs published by a major record label


* Write and record at least one song per week


* Attend songwriting workshops or classes to improve skills and learn new techniques


* Collaborate with other songwriters or musicians to gain new perspectives and ideas


* Research and study successful songwriters to understand what makes their songs popular


* Build a portfolio of original songs and record high-quality demos


* Network with industry professionals such as music producers, publishers, and A&R representatives


* Submit songs to music contests or festivals to gain exposure and recognition


* Create a website or social media presence to showcase songs and connect with fans


* Attend live music events and performances to stay up-to-date on current trends and styles


* Stay persistent and committed to the goal, even in the face of rejection or setbacks.
